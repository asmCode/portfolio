Feel The Vision - Support system for blind people
Event
Microsoft Imagine Cup 2011, category Embedded Development

Achievment
Second qualifications round

Abstract
Project Feel The Vision is intended for blind people. It can recognize positions and distances of surrounding objects and pass data to the user, who can easily interpret this information without additional effort. System also extends GPS system to be more friendly for blind people.

My work
Designed and implemented most of the software part, designed and assembled band, created documentation, video

Technology
C#, WCF, WPF, Silverlight, ASP, MSSQL2008, GPS, eBox 3300

Hand - Tool for manual practice
Event
Microsoft Imagine Cup 2007, category Software Design

Achievment
Poland finalist

Abstract
Our solution will enable people to gain more knowledge about the practical side of science. To practice those, one needs specialistic equipment and the environment of these practises is impossible to be arranged in one's home. Hair-dressers, dentists or welder will be able to familiarise themselves with their job in the virtual world, using a tool held in their hands – as if it was happening for real.

My work
Designed and implemented most application, assembled hardware, created documentation, video

Technology
C#, DirectX


Isis - System for safe and comfortable pregnancy
Event
Microsoft Imagine Cup 2009, category Software Design

Achievment
1st Place at Poland and 4th place on World Finals in Cario!! We defeated over 70 countries!

Abstract
ISIS is a project which aims for decreasing mortality rate of newborns. To speak briefly it is modern, specialized medical system which can be deployed on regular PCs. Low costs and easiness of use makes it an ideal solution both for individual customers and institutions involved in organizing field hospitals, refugee camps etc.

My work
Designed and implemented most of the application, created documentation, video

Technology
C#, WCF, WPF, Silverlight, ASP, MSSQL2008, C++, Direct Media, fMod


LifeTracker - Real-time telemetric system
Event
Microsoft Imagine Cup 2008, category Software Design

Achievment
1st place at Poland and finalists at World Finals at Paris

Abstract
LifeTracker is a real time telemetry system that helps to protect animal species better. We created a system which incorporates many available tools and innovative solutions in attempt to enhance telemetry monitoring of animals. LifeTracker, a fully automatic system, brings us real time data about the position of tracked animal in three dimensional space.

My work
Designed and implemented whole application, created documentation, video

Technology
C#, DirectX, MSSQL


Reacto - Rehabilitation system
Event
Microsoft Imagine Cup 2010, category Software Design

Achievment
Poland finalist

Abstract
Reacto is a project which mainly aids rehabilitation of people afflicted with psychophysical disabilities, such as autism and cerebral palsy. It also finds application in overcoming various types of phobias, for example claustrophobia, arachnophobia and fear of heights.

My work
Designed and implemented augmented reality engine, graphics engine, created documentation, video

Technology
C#, XNA, opengl, WCF, WPF, Multitouch, MSSQL
